Whether it's a gift for someone special or a splashy item for yourself, Snygg always has something unique.

All items are created from repurposed, vintage, or remnant fabrics. It may have been a fantastic flannel
or your grandmother's curtain in a previous life, but now it's been re-imagined for a new use.

About the creator:

Inga is a graphic designer with an addiction to fabric. She loves combining colors and patterns and enjoys making anything old new again. In the computer world, she has worked in the Chicago area for over 10 years, with many talented folks, most recently with those at Northwestern University and John G. Shedd Aquarium. In the sewing world, she merely wishes to create and explore, and see where life takes her.
